Dear Prospective Dartmouth Fencer:


Whether you are a seasoned fencer or a beginner who has yet to step foot on the strip, let us be the first to say--welcome home. We are thrilled by your interest in DCFC, and we want to take the time here to tell you a little more about our team.

You are coming at an exciting time. Dartmouth Fencing is riding the wave of two successful, historic seasons. In 2014, we won silver in men's épée, gold in men's team overall, and gold in men's and women's combined team in the USACFC College Fencing National Championships. In 2015, we won gold in women's foil and gold in best women's team overall.

Here's where you come into the picture: at the core of DCFC are the people. We all coach each other, support one another, cheer everyone on, and push our teammates to the next level. We are always on the lookout for motivated people to join our team. No experience is necessary! As long as you are interested in fencing and willing to put in the time, we want YOU on our team!



What to expect?

 
  • Practices with drills and open fencing

  • Crazy (but safe!) road trips to competitions

  • Team dinners, pumpkin picking, cabin trips, midnight breakfasts, etc.

  • A strong community that will be here for you for the rest of your time in college (and beyond!)
