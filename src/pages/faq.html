Frequently Asked Questions


  • Q: What is Dartmouth Fencing? How can I find out more information / join the team?
    A: Check out the "Join Us" page and the "History" page!

  • Q: What is the commitment? What if I can't make every practice every day? Can I still join?
    A: Practices are mandatory, but we do understand people may have special circumstances. Practice can be occasionally missed as long as captains are notified in advance. If you have a regular conflict, such as another club or commitment, please talk to the captains prior to joining in order to work things out.

  • Q: How do I contact DCFC?
    A: All of our contact information can be found on the "Join Us" page. We can also be reached at "Fencing Club, 6083 Alumni Gym, Dartmouth College, Hanover, NH, 03755‚Äù.

  • Q: Do I need to have my own equipment or already know how to fence in order to join?
    A: Not at all! Many of our members had zero experience fencing prior to college. We don't require you to purchase your own equipment, and we provide club equipment for people who need it.

  • Q: How can I donate to DCFC? Any other info about supporting DCFC?
    A: Donations can be sent to our post address, which is "Fencing Club, 6084 Alumni Gym, Dartmouth College, Hanover, NH, 04755". Check out our "Support" page to find out more.

  • Q: Can I drop in and fence with you guys if I'm visiting and not on the team?
    A: This depends on the season and who is currently on campus. Contact the captains!

  • Q: Where can I get the most up-to-date information and news about the team?
    A: We monitor and update this website regularly. Check our home page and "News" page for recent DCFC events! You can also follow our Facebook or Instagram.

  • Q: I have a question that isn't listed here, what can I do?
    A: Contact the captains via email at fencing.club {at} dartmouth.edu! (Replace the {at} with @). We are always happy to answer any questions regarding the team or fencing. We also love talking to prospective students/fencers!
